Some tips to reduce Your stress

  • Try not to worry about the future or compare yourself with others.
  • Try to plan your time to help you keep track of your work. Break it down into manageable chunks so you can keep up with deadlines.
  • Try talking to a friend, tutor or someone in your family about your stress.
  • Balance work and play.
  • Plan your day,Stick to your plan.
  • Get enough sleep,Exercise every day.
  • Go for a walk
  • Watch a comedy,Dance
  • Cook Something delicious
  • Take a bath or shower (wash your hair!)
  • Play with a pet, toddler or child
  • Do a random act of kindness
  • Write out your dream life
  • Spend a day doing things that make you happy
  • Punch a pillow
  • Call someone to let them know you love and appreciate them.
  • Listen to music
  • Have face-to-face interaction with family and friends.
  • Limit your time on technological devices. Strive to be off all tech products 1-2 hours before bed.
  • Surround yourself with positive words and images.
  • Take a quick nap. (Try not to nap after 3 PM, so you do not interfere with your sleep schedule)
  • Say no to things you do not enjoy, and yes to the things that bring you joy.
  • Engage in breathing exercises before bed to improve sleep and reduce night time anxiety.
  • Eat on a consistent schedule – even if you don’t feel hungry put something in your body for nutrients.
  • Avoid tobacco products and alcohol
  • Limit access to people and things that cause you stress.
  • Color in a coloring book.
